Force Layouts

Allowable Datasets

  1. Road maps would work well in a force layout because intersections would be represented by nodes and the rodes would be the edges connecting those intersections.
  2. Family trees could also be done well using force layouts. Each family member would be a node with their relationships to the other people, the edges.
  3. It would also be good for viewing a friendship web. It would offer insights into which of each nodes friends are friends and how closely people are. Each person would be a node, with their edges being their friends.
